{%- comment -%} "Đọc tiếp" — the end of one article offers the next one (philosophy §5). The reader who reaches the bottom of a post is the reader most worth keeping: they just proved they read to the end. This block hands them up to three more articles in the compact thumb-and-title rows every feed has taught them to binge on. Order of preference: 1. other posts on the SAME TOPIC, newest first — "more like what you just finished" is the strongest next read; 2. then the newest posts from the rest of the hub, to fill up to three. Posts in the SAME SERIES are excluded: the series nav directly above already offers those, and offering a post twice on one screen reads as machinery.
